Nail design

Nail design is one of my best skills and I enjoy doing it a lot. It makes me happy that I am so skilled and good at it. I have a crazy amount of supplies and I like that it is completly customizable. I am able to express my creativity through nail art.

How to gain the skill!

It may feel discouraging after trying this skill and not getting the output you wanted but that is okay! When I started I was not good but after like a year of practice and a steady hand i was able to make some amazing things. I have a plethera of brushes, polishes, tools, and other detailing things. Sometimes I use insperation pictures but other times I just make them up on my own with my own creativity!
