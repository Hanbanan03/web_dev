Bear Lake

Bear lake is a beutiful place on the border of Utah and Idaho and is great for a staycation if you're near those areas! When I go there for stacations I enjoy paddleboarding on the cool waters and having picnics by the beach. It is so much fun to get shakes from there famous places!

Local Attractions

  • Get a Raspberry shake!
  • Go boating or paddleboarding
  • Go on amazing hikes
  • Have a picnic by the beach
  • Shop at the cute local shops

Bear Lake has super blue water and lots of fun things to do, like swimming and boating. Many people go there in the summertime to relax and explore.
